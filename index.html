Viativa
via negativa · the path through subtraction
Viativa is built on the principle of via negativa — the idea that we often find the right path not by adding more, but by removing what harms us. Rather than telling you what to eat, Viativa helps you identify what to avoid based on your own body's signals.
What Viativa does
Upload your IgG food sensitivity test results or enter your known restrictions. Viativa reads your profile and uses AI to analyze any dish or restaurant menu, flagging ingredients that may trigger a reaction for you specifically.
Important disclaimer
Viativa is designed for food sensitivities and dietary optimization — not life-threatening allergies. IgG reactions are delayed immune responses that cause discomfort and inflammation, not anaphylaxis. If you have a severe allergy (peanuts, shellfish, etc.), always verify ingredients directly with the restaurant or food manufacturer. Viativa's AI analysis is a helpful guide, not a medical guarantee. Always consult your doctor or dietitian for medical dietary advice.
How your data is stored
Your profile is stored securely in the cloud and identified by a unique ID on your device. No name or personal information is required. Your data is never shared or sold.
